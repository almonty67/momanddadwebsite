Drawing Supply List
  • Pencils: 2 each of Graphite Pencils 2H, 2B, 4B, and 6B
  • Charcoal: 2 each of Charcoal Pencils 2B (hard), 4B (medium), 6B (extra soft)
  • Paper: 1 pad of 18” x 24” drawing paper (not sketch paper) 1 pad of lined paper used for note taking
  • Kneaded Eraser
  • Ruler
  • String and a plain washer (used to make a plumb line) Knitting Needle
  • Paper Towels (prefer Viva Brand)
  • Paper Stumps (package of various sizes)

You will be learning to draw from still various still lives constructed by the instructor (not from photos). You will learn to measure, construct a professional composition, and will use a method called the sight size method used originally by the French Academies. You will also learn linear and atmospheric perspective and how these two disciplines play in constructing a drawing. This course requires a certain discipline in that you will be expected to spend drawing every day when not in class.

Oil Painting Supply List
Colors: ( Brand: Golden for Acrylics and Lukas for Oil works best)
  • Primary Yellow (Lemon yellow)
  • Primary Blue (Cyan)
  • Primary Red (Magenta) (Windsor Newton does not work) Cadmium Yellow Lite
  • Indian Yellow
  • Cadmium Orange
  • Cadmium Red Lite
  • Alizarin Crimson
  • Dioxazine Purple
  • Ultramarine Blue
  • Phthalo Blue
  • Phthalo Green
  • Titanium White
  • Burnt Umber
  • Brushes: Number 6, 8, and 12 Bright and same numbers in Filberts A one inch or number 20 Bright for background painting.
  • Medium and Canvas or Boards:
    • Turpenoid for brush cleaning and thinning of paint (oil painting only)
    • Brush cleaner container (oil painting only)
    • Alkyd medium – small bottle (oil painting only)
    • 6 16x20 inch canvas or boards
  • General equipment:
    • Paper towels and/or rags
    • Palette knives (one long spatula type-very flexible for mixing) Palette
    • Palette paper if you prefer

Please wear clothes that you use for painting!!

Note: Be prepared to use floor easels and a Taboret (table).
